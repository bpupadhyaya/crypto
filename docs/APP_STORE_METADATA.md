## Long Description

Open Wallet is a free, open-source, non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet that puts you in complete control of your digital assets. Your private keys never leave your device.

**Multi-Chain Support**
Manage Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Cosmos, and Open Chain tokens from a single wallet. More chains coming soon.

**Swap & Bridge**
Exchange tokens directly within the app using built-in swap options including atomic swaps, DEX trading, and order book matching. Bridge assets across blockchains via IBC, Li.Fi, and THORChain integration.

**Privacy First**
No analytics. No tracking. No accounts. No data collection. Open Wallet operates entirely on your device. Verify every line of code yourself — the full source is public.

**Security by Design**
- Non-custodial: only you hold your keys
- Encrypted vault with Argon2 key derivation
- Biometric authentication (Face ID / fingerprint)
- PIN backup for all operations
- Paper trading system to practice before using real funds
- Mandatory safety checks before real-money transactions

**Simple & Pro Modes**
New to crypto? Start with Simple mode for a clean, straightforward experience. Ready for more? Switch to Pro mode for full control over fees, chains, and advanced features.

**Open Source**
Built in the open. Every component — wallet, chain integrations, swap engines — is fully auditable. No hidden code, no proprietary backends.

**On-Ramp Support**
Buy crypto with fiat currency through trusted on-ramp providers (MoonPay, Transak, Ramp) directly within the app.
